galaxy_dive.utils.astro.get_sneii_metal_budget(m_star, y_z_ii=0.03)[source]¶

Get the total mass of metal produced by SNe by z=0 in a galaxy with a z=0 stellar mass of m_star. This is taken from Peeple+2014, who use the star formation histories of Leitner+2012.

Parameters:
  • m_star (float or array-like) – Mass of the target galaxy at z=0 in units of Msun.
  • y_z_ii (float) – Nucleosynthetic yield of all heavy
Returns:

Units of Msun.
Return type:

sneii_metal_budget (same as m_star)
